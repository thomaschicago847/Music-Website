    • Beginner Trumpet Lessons
      Beginner trumpet lessons are designed to introduce the student to the world of brass music in an engaging and fun way. During these lessons, the student will learn the fundamental techniques and principles of playing the trumpet. This includes understanding the trumpet’s design, proper posture, breath control, basic fingerings, and producing your first notes. These lessons will guide the student in building a strong foundation to set the stage for their musical growth.
    • Intermediate Trumpet Lessons
      These lessons will delve deeper into the art of trumpet playing. You will refine your embouchure, develop a wider range, and gain proficiency in scales and arpeggios. These lessons will introduce you to more complex music theory concepts and expand your repertoire, allowing you to explore various musical styles.
    • Advanced Trumpet Lessons
      For the advanced trumpet player, these lessons are tailored to prepare you for auditions, competitions, and performances. These lessons will focus on intricate technical exercises, and advanced scales and arpeggios to acclimate you to more advanced repertoire that you will see in the professional environment. These lessons will explore challenging repertoire, including orchestral excerpts, classical solos, contemporary compositions, and improvisation. These lessons will also delve into the nuances of performance and expression, music business, and the tools you need to be successful in your musical career.
    • What To Expect
      Personalized Instruction. Regardless of your level, these lessons are meant to address the student's specific strengths and areas for improvement.

      Technique and Tone. One of Chicago's many treasures is the Chicago Symphony Orchestra with its rich history of brass playing. These lessons seek to teach the same technique and tone production used by these world class musicians.

      Music Theory. Learn essential music theory concepts.

      Performance Opportunities. Whether you aspire to perform in a school band, a professional orchestra, or as a solo artist, these lessons will prepare you for a variety of musical experiences.
