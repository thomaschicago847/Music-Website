  • Experience
    Thomas is currently a freelance musician in Chicago. Most recently he can be heard playing trumpet at his friends’ weddings, playing organ at Mass in Chicago, or singing with his band called Pickled Herring. Thomas began playing trumpet at the age of 10 years old. In high school and college he studied and performed in Chicago and Los Angeles. Having served as principal trumpet of the Chicago Youth Symphony Orchestra, he gained a deep love for classical music and dedication to learning trumpet. His passion for trumpet has taken him to Italy, where he toured after college with his high school’s concert band and jazz band as a featured soloist. Notable teachers include Bryan Majure, Chris O’Hara, and Boyde Hood.
  • Military Experience
    SGT Martin is a military intelligence non-commissioned officer. He has served 6 years in the United States Army Reserves. In 2019, SGT Martin was deployed to Jordan in support of Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF) and is a Veteran. SGT Martin now joyfully serves at his unit based in the northwest suburbs of Chicago. SGT Martin contributes to the mission of the 88th Readiness Divisions Casualty Operations, pallbearing deceased Soldiers and performing Live Taps on Bugle to honor our nation’s Veterans at Military Funeral Honors services. SGT Martin is a certified Combat Lifesaver.
